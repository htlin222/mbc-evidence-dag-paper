## Step-by-step submission flow

1. **Visit** https://www.medrxiv.org/submit-a-manuscript
2. **Sign in** or create account (uses ORCID)
3. **Select** "New Submission" → fill author info (use `medrxiv_metadata.md`)
4. **Upload** `paper_B_methods.pdf` AND `paper_B_methods.docx`
5. **Paste** the abstract from `medrxiv_metadata.md` into the abstract field
6. **Enter** keywords (6 keywords from `medrxiv_metadata.md`)
7. **Select** subject area: Health Informatics (primary), Oncology (secondary)
8. **Confirm** data availability statement
9. **Add** competing-interests and funding statements
10. **Submit** for medRxiv staff screening (typically 1–3 days)

## After acceptance

- medRxiv assigns a permanent DOI (format `10.1101/2026.xx.xx.xxxxxxxx`)
- This DOI goes into Paper A's cover letter to JCO PO as "previously deposited as preprint"

Per the strategic plan:
- **Lower risk path**: medRxiv accepts methods papers in oncology informatics without confirmatory rejection; no peer review at deposit time
- **Establishes priority** for the framework name, schema, and EFDPR statistic
- **Provides citable DOI** for Paper A's submission to JCO PO 2 weeks later
- **Surfaces feedback** from the community before formal peer review
